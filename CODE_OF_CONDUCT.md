# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We commit to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give technical feedback with clarity and professionalism.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting
Open a confidential issue titled `conduct-report` or contact the maintainer directly.
